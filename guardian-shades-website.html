How Guardian Shades Work

Simple, intuitive, and always ready to protect you whenever and wherever you need it.

1

Wear Your Guardian Shades

Simply wear your stylish Guardian Shades like any regular sunglasses. The discreet camera and sensors automatically activate when worn.

2

AI Monitors Your Surroundings

The advanced AI continuously scans your environment, analyzing behavioral patterns to identify potential threats or suspicious activities.

When a potential threat is detected, you'll receive a discreet audio alert through the built-in earpiece. You can also manually activate protection features through the companion app.

In case of danger, Guardian Shades automatically alert your emergency contacts with your GPS location, activate the alarm, and prepare the self-defense spray for use if needed.
